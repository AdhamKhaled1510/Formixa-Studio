Services Crafted for

From concept sketches to photorealistic renders, we offer end-to-end 3D design services tailored to your project's demands.

Our capabilities

3D Modeling & Sculpting

Parametric and freeform 3D models built to exact tolerances. From industrial components to organic art forms, we translate ideas into precise digital geometry.

  • Parametric & NURBS modeling
  • Organic sculpting and retopology
  • CAD-ready export formats (STEP, OBJ, FBX)
  • Tolerance verification reports

Rendering & Visualization

Photorealistic imagery and animations that bring your 3D assets to life — suitable for client presentations, marketing campaigns, and virtual production.

  • Studio & environment lighting setups
  • Material and texture authoring
  • Still renders up to 8K resolution
  • Animated walkthroughs & turntables

Product Design & Prototyping

We partner with product teams to design manufacturable geometries and create digital prototypes ready for 3D printing, CNC machining, or injection molding.

  • Design-for-manufacture (DFM) reviews
  • 3D print-ready file preparation
  • Material & finish simulation
  • Iteration support across rounds

Design Consultation

Expert creative direction from concept to completion. We help you define the visual language of your project and ensure every design decision is purposeful.

  • Project scoping & creative briefs
  • Reference & moodboard development
  • Design system documentation
  • Ongoing creative advisory retainers
