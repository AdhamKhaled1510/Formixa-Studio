Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?
Most Starter projects complete in 7–10 days. Studio projects average 2–3 weeks depending on scope. Enterprise timelines are scoped during onboarding. We'll always give you a realistic schedule before work begins.
What file formats do you deliver?
We deliver in any format you need — OBJ, FBX, STEP, STL, GLTF/GLB for 3D assets; PNG, EXR, TIFF for renders; MP4/MOV for animations. Just let us know your pipeline requirements.
Do you work with clients remotely?
Absolutely — the majority of our clients are based outside Portugal. We work across time zones and have a structured remote collaboration process including shared project portals, video check-ins, and async feedback tools.
Who owns the final files and IP?
Upon final payment, full intellectual property transfers to you. We retain the right to display work in our portfolio unless you request a confidentiality clause, which is standard in our Enterprise agreements.
Can I request revisions after delivery?
Each plan includes a set number of revision rounds. After those are used, additional revisions are billed at an hourly rate. We make sure you're fully briefed on this before any work begins so there are no surprises.
