Who we are

Built on Precision.
Driven by Vision.

Formixa Studio is a design collective dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in three-dimensional space.

Our story

From a Shared Obsession with Form and Space

Founded in 2014 by a team of architects and digital artists who believed that great 3D design could be both technically rigorous and emotionally resonant, Formixa Studio has grown into a studio trusted by brands, product engineers, and creative directors worldwide.

We don't just model geometry — we build experiences. Every project begins with deep listening, moves through careful research, and culminates in a design that surprises even the client who commissioned it.

Our team spans disciplines: structural engineers, visual artists, material scientists, and software developers — all united by a love for precision and an appetite for the unexpected.
