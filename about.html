Our story

From a Shared Obsession with Form and Space

Formixa Studio is a fresh design venture launched in 2026, built on a deep passion for 3D craft. What started as a one-person obsession with form and space is now a focused studio ready to take on challenging, meaningful projects.

We don't just model geometry — we build experiences. Every project begins with deep listening, moves through careful research, and culminates in a design that surprises even the client who commissioned it.

Solo but not limited — combining technical rigor, artistic sensibility, and genuine curiosity to deliver work that stands out.
